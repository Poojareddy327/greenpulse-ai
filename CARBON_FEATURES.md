# 🌍 Carbon Footprint Awareness Platform - Enhanced Features

## New Carbon-Focused Features Added ✅

### 1. 🛒 **Carbon Offset Marketplace** (`/offsets`)
Purchase carbon credits to offset your emissions through verified climate projects.

**Features:**
- **Quick Offset Packages**: 1, 5, or 10 ton packages with discounts
- **Verified Projects**:
  - 🌳 Reforestation (Amazon) - $12/ton
  - 💨 Wind Energy (Texas) - $15/ton
  - ☀️ Solar Power (India) - $14/ton
  - 🌊 Ocean Cleanup (Pacific) - $18/ton
- **Shopping Cart**: Add projects and checkout
- **Certification**: Gold Standard, VCS, Blue Carbon verified
- **Real Impact Tracking**: See exactly what your money achieves

**How It Works:**
1. Calculate your carbon footprint
2. Purchase offset credits
3. Support verified climate projects
4. Receive certificate

---

### 2. 📊 **Carbon Comparison Tool** (`/comparison`)
Compare your carbon footprint with global and regional averages.

**Features:**
- **Global Averages by Region**:
  - United States: 16,000 kg CO₂/year
  - EU: 7,000 kg CO₂/year  
  - China: 8,000 kg CO₂/year
  - India: 1,900 kg CO₂/year
  - Paris Agreement Target: 2,000 kg CO₂/year

- **Real-World Equivalents**:
  - 100 kg CO₂ = 400 km driving
  - 1 Ton CO₂ = 1 transatlantic flight
  - 1 Ton = 40 trees needed to absorb

- **Activity Carbon Values**:
  - Beef (1 kg) = 27 kg CO₂
  - Chicken (1 kg) = 6.9 kg CO₂
  - Gasoline (1 L) = 2.3 kg CO₂
  - Flight (1000 km) = 250 kg CO₂
  - Train (1000 km) = 14 kg CO₂
